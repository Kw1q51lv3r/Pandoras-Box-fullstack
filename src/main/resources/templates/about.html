PANDORA'S BOX

a concept by afterburner

OUR MISSION

Household supplies are crucial in maintaining both a healthy life and a healthy home, so it makes sense to lower the barrier to obtaining them as much as possible. Pandora’s Box was created as a way of getting these essentials in an easy, streamlined, and convenient manner, reducing waste. Less time fussing at the shops, more time for you.

ABOUT AFTERBURNER

Team Afterburner was formed out of a desire to burn ahead of the competition on easy provisions of valuable home supplies in the new normal. Together, they crossed swords on the purpose of maintaining sustainable and reliable services, prioritising stability and building strong foundations.
